About Us

Some details about our passionate team that runs the company.

Paul Yang

CEO

Paul Yang is the founder of In Sequence Software LLC and works on the mobile versions of the various apps. Originally from Taiwan, he has developed a passion for making child and pet adoption easier and more accessible to all families while growing up in the United States. His technical interests are in mobile development and machine learning.

Jason Yang

Software Engineering Intern

Jason Yang is a software intern of In Sequence Software LLC and works on the websites and mobile versions of the various apps. After volunteering in his community, he has learned about the trouble and importance of connecting people with unique needs together. His technical interests are in web design, big data, and machine learning.

Vivian Lai

Co-Founder

Vivian Lai is a full-stack developer of In Sequence Software LLC and works on the web version of Pet Adopter using React. She wishes to adopt a cat or dog in the future to help her community out. She is interested in React and web technologies.

Lani Viet

Animator

Lani Viet is the animator of In Sequence Software LLC and is the artist behind the animated video of Pet Adopter. She uses Medibang Paint Pro to draw the illustrations and Pencil 2D to construct animations. She is interested in playing tennis.
