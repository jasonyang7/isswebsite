Family Connection

Enables pregnant women to easily connect with adoption professionals, social workers, adoption attorneys, or other families to help their adoption process.

Download on iOS Download on Android

The Only App You Need for Your Adoption

Family Connection allows adoptive families to talk to pregnant women considering or who have decided on adoption. Adoptive families have a lot of difficulty finding pregnant women considering adoption, and this website is to make it easier for them.

Easily Find and Match with Others

Adoptive families can chat with pregnant women considering adoption. Adoptive families go through a screening process before being able to chat with pregnant women. Pregnant women considering adoption will be able to talk to adoptive families who have been vetted, and are waiting to adopt.

Initiating Contact with Matches

Easily interact with other individuals who have gone through the screening process once matched.

Chat and Find Information

Once matched with a trusted user, you can look at their profile, chat, and take a look at their location.
