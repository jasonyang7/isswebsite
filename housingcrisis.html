Housing Crisis

A simple app enabling you to save money by renting out specific rooms in their homes.

Download on iOS Download on Android

Your all-in-one app to rent and rent out rooms.

Rent out extra rooms in your home. Ideal for situations where you have additional rooms in your house you'd like to rent out. Save money by renting from willing renters.

Easily Find and Match with Others

With a swipe to the right or a press of the heart button, you are easily matched with a potential renter or rentee.

Initiating Contact with Matches

Once you are matched with another person, you can interact with the person.

Chat and Find Information

Some interactions include looking at their profile, chatting (swiping up), and taking a look at their location.
