Pet Adopter

The go-to app for adopting other pets or putting your own pets up for adoption.

Download on iOS Download on Android

The optimal app to adopt pets and put pets up for adoption.

We believe that pet adoption can go directly from owner to owner. Animal shelters or pet rescues are often full and cannot take in more pets. Direct pet adoption means that the pet will receive more individual attention instead of living in the shelter. Many people want to adopt pets, but often find pet stores or animal shelters to be expensive or too far away. It is easy to find pet owners or pet adopters nearby through this app. However, animal shelters and rescues can still advertise their pets on this app.

Easily Find and Match with Others

Find others in your areas that will be suitable to adopt your pet or pets to adopt.

Initiating Contact with Matches

View others' profiles to decide if they are a suitable new owner for your pet or have a good pet you want to adopt.

Chat and Find Information

Chat with the person who currently owns the pet. This way, you will find out if the pet is right for you. You can ask about the pet's history, behaviors, and other important information.
