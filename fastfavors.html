Fast Favors

Find and give help for your everyday tasks to other users, such as walking a dog, watering plants, or waiting in line.

Download on Android

The essential app to help and give help to others.

Fast Favors is the app that lets you find or give help for your everyday micro tasks. A micro task is one that people don't mind doing for free, and don't mind doing for free. Get help with waiting in line, taking out your trash, picking up your packages, feeding your dog, watering your plants or anything that is quick and only takes a few minutes.

Easily Find and Match with Others

Helpers can find each other by looking at their profiles, and if they are interested, the helpers can swipe right on each other to initiate the helping process.

Initiating Contact with Matches

Easily interact with other individuals who want to give help to others once matched.

Chat and Find Information

Once the other user swipes right on your profile, you become matched with a user. You can look at their profile, chat, and take a look at their location.
